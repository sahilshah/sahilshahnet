Hello World!

I am Sahil, a 24 year old engineer at Apple Inc. I work in the Special Projects Group there on computer vision problems. Until December 2015, I was a grad student at the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. Before that, I studied Computer Science at BITS Pilani in India and worked for a year at Tonbo Imaging on computer vision problems in the defense sector. I am a programmer and love building systems in the areas of Artificial Intelligence (primarily computer vision and machine learning), Internet of Things and Automation.

Also, I was a James R. Swartz Entrepreneurial Fellow at CMU. This fellowship is awarded to 12 graduate students across CMU who are interested in entrepreneurship and wish to startup in the future. It is sponsored by Jim Swartz, the founder of Accel partners.

I am an avid reader and love to read fiction and various other topics like science, philosophy and entreprenurship. I am a sports maniac (play not watch!) and a distance runner in the making. My favourite sports are Basketball and Squash. To know more about my work check out my projects or my Linkedin profile.
